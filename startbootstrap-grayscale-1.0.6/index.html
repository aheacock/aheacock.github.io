About Me

I am a computer engineering student at Virginia Tech. I love engineering, martial arts, music, and photography.

As a computer engineer I spend most of my time developing in C/C++ and Python. I also have experience working with the REDHAWK software defined radio framework.

I have been involved in Martial Arts for over 10 years, and am currently a 3rd degree black belt, or Sam-Dan. I am certified as a Head Instructor and as a Tournament Judge by the International Tang Soo Do Federation.

I have been interested in music my entire life, and have played the trumpet for more than 12 years.

Photography is another passion of mine, here is a link to my Flickr portfolio.
